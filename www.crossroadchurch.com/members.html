Meet Our Members!

We have some important members, that build the church up from the ground. We love them, as much as we love you. Join us, and meet some of them. Do not worry, they do not bite!


Herman Martins

Senior Pastor

Herman has been with this church for about 20 years, and his faith has never changed. He used to be a follower, but with his dedication and undying belief, he rose up, and became senior pastor. He and his wife, Janine, have 3 children.


Josiah

Senior Pastor

Josiah Rogers is a man with great faith. He has brought many people over to us, so that we could help them with their journey to salvation. He himself is going through some tough times, but he's improving through the help of the teachings.


Silas Peyton

Pastor

Silas is a hardworking man, and has made many great friends through this church. He has been with this church for 5 years, and has raised many great questions, to strengthen his faith. He is a strong believer and a good friend to people around him. He and his wife, Jeanne, has been married for 2 years, and have one child.


Erin Cooper

Deacon

Erin has been a dedicated member of our church, and so is his wife, Gwendoline. They both got married in 2015. Both of them are part of the worship team, working side by side. One thing we can be sure is that their love has not unwavered, and never will.


Gwendoline Brice

Minister Of Finance

A dedicated member of our church, and a wonderful finance manager. Previously, she used to work in a bank, and is now working for the church, to manage our finances and budgeting issues.


Faye Jones

Minister of Education

Faye is a lovely member, and has been with us since the start. She has been amazing about her faith, and loudly proclaims it no matter what. She has been shunned for her faith, but she has found comfort and a new family with us. Now, she plans the teachings for everyone.


Cheyanne Granville

Minister of Music and Performing Arts

Cheyanne used to direct music and plays, and even made some herself. Now, she spreads the word in the way she knows the best, music. She teaches everyone about her faith and love, and wants to bring everyone to a better future.


Rosaline Simmons

Executive Leader of Ministries

Rosaline used to be an investigative journalist, and has made many friends and contacts in her life. However, due to complicated relationships and unrequited love, she has found a new home with us, and now helps to get more friends and ministries to work together with us.


Diana Sauter

Youth Pastor

Teaching children is not easy, but with Diana, we are able to teach many. Diana was a teacher, and used to teach in all types of schools. Now, she teaches the younger kids about what faith is, and how we should learn and strive to reach salvation.
