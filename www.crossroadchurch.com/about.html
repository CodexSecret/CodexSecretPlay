Our Mission


"Convalescence is a sort of grown-up rebirth, enabling us to see life with a fresh eye."
-Margaret Prescott Montague


We want to...

  1. Help you on your enlightenment journey. We know that life isn't as easy, and we'd like to help you through your problems.
  2. Be there for you. We want to be able to hear your problems, and care for you, and understand what you're going through.
  3. Teach you more. We want to show you enlightenment, and teach you more about what is right and what is wrong under her eyes.
  4. Help you to grow mature. We want you to grow, and learn the way to understand and deal with life. We will be there for you, but we want you to grow and learn to make the right decisions under her eyes.
  5. Connect you to people with similar experiences, and for them to be able to learn from you, or for you to learn from them.
  6. Share your experiences with others, and to teach others on your victory against those problems you had. Everyone will be able to learn from them.
  7. Help you through the temptations, and teach you to resist them. Temptations are a part of life that we cannot remove, and we'd like to help you to curb those temptations.
  8. Bring you to Salvation.
