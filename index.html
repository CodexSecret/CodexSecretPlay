What is it?

There's many names for what we have here. An Online Escape
Room, an Alternate Reality Game, a Puzzle Game. There's a lot
of things that you could call this. We try our best to self contain
whatever puzzle you get. Which means that outside tools are
not necessary. They can all be solved by hand, or by mind!

Choose your adventure!

First, all you have to do is choose your adventure based off what
you like! Whether it'd be saving a girl, fighting mystical creatures, or
finding the secrets on a mystical island, we always welcome you to
join the adventure. To certain death, peril, or victory...

Prepare!

You may ask, what should I prepare? Nothing! Just prepare yourself
for the ride of a life! And if you like to write, get a pen and paper. It
always helps!

Solve and complete your mission!

Solve the puzzles in front of you, interact with characters, and
access highly detailed websites, and clues. Maybe you will be the
one to accomplish in the face of adversity!
